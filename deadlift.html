Deadlift Guide
Step 1: Setup

Stand with your feet hip-width apart, toes pointing forward. Place the barbell over the middle of your feet and adjust it to your shin's proximity.

Step 2: Grip the Bar

Bend at your hips and knees to lower your body and grip the barbell with both hands. Use a grip slightly wider than shoulder-width with palms facing you or mixed grip.

Step 3: Set Your Back

Keep your back straight, chest up, and shoulder blades pulled back. Engage your core to maintain a neutral spine throughout the lift.

Step 4: Lift Off

Begin the lift by driving through your heels, extending your hips and knees simultaneously. Keep the bar close to your body as you stand up.

Step 5: Lockout

Stand tall with your shoulders back and chest up. Fully extend your hips and knees, locking out at the top of the movement.
