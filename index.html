We Offers This exercises

Push-ups:

Muscles Worked: Chest, shoulders, triceps, and core.

Description: Start in a plank position, lower your body to the ground by bending your elbows, and then push back up to the starting position.

Start Now
Squats:

Muscles Worked: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and lower back.

Description: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body by bending your knees, keeping your back straight, and then push back up.

Start Now
Plank:

Muscles Worked: Core, shoulders, and lower back.

Description: Hold a push-up position with your arms straight, body forming a straight line from head to heels, and engage your core muscles.

Start Now
Lunges:

Muscles Worked: Quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves.

Description: Step forward with one leg, lower your body until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle, then push back up and repeat with the other leg.

Start Now
Pull-ups:

Muscles Worked: Back, biceps, and forearms.

Description: Hang from a horizontal bar with palms facing away, pull your body up until your chin passes the bar, then lower yourself back down.

Start Now
Deadlifts:

Muscles Worked: Lower back, hamstrings, glutes, and core.

Description: Stand with feet hip-width apart, bend at the hips and knees to lower the weights, then stand back up, keeping your back straight.

Start Now
Burpees:

Muscles Worked: Full-body workout.

Description: Start in a standing position, drop into a squat position, kick your feet back into a plank position, immediately return your feet into squat position, and jump up from the squat position.

Start Now
Mountain Climbers:

Muscles Worked: Core, shoulders, and legs.

Description: Start in a plank position, bring one knee towards your chest, then switch legs in a running motion while maintaining a plank position.

Start Now
