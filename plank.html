Plank Guide
Step 1: Starting Position

Begin in a forearm plank position with your elbows directly below your shoulders. Maintain a straight line from your head to your heels.

Step 2: Core Engagement

Engage your core by pulling your navel towards your spine. Keep your back flat, and avoid sagging or lifting your hips.

Step 3: Elbow and Toe Alignment

Ensure your elbows are positioned directly beneath your shoulders. Keep your toes on the ground, creating a straight line with your body.

Step 4: Hold and Breathe

Maintain the plank position, holding it for the desired duration. Focus on steady breathing throughout the exercise.

Step 5: Modification

For beginners, consider starting with a modified plank on your knees. Gradually progress to a full plank as your strength increases.
