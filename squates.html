Squat Guide
Step 1: Starting Position

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly turned outwards. Keep your spine neutral, chest up, and core engaged.

Step 2: Descend

Initiate the movement by bending your knees and hips simultaneously. Lower your body as if you're sitting back into a chair, keeping your chest lifted.

Step 3: Depth

Lower yourself until your thighs are parallel to the ground or slightly below. Ensure your knees track in line with your toes, not caving inward.

Step 4: Ascend

Drive through your heels to push yourself back up to the starting position. Keep your chest up and maintain a strong core throughout the movement.

Step 5: Finish

Stand tall at the top of the movement, fully extending your hips and knees. Squeeze your glutes at the top to maximize engagement.
