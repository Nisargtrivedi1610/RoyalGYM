Mountain Climber Guide
Step 1: Starting Position

Begin in a plank position with your hands directly under your shoulders. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.

Step 2: Drive Knee to Chest

Bring your right knee towards your chest, maintaining a strong core. Keep your hips level and your shoulders directly above your hands.

Step 3: Extend Right Leg

Extend your right leg back to the plank position, maintaining control. Your body should remain in a straight line.

Step 4: Repeat on Left Side

Bring your left knee towards your chest, then extend the left leg back to plank. Continue alternating legs in a rhythmic fashion.

Step 5: Maintain Pace

Keep a steady pace and engage your core throughout the exercise. Aim for a controlled and dynamic movement.
